Visit Fontainebleau!

Enjoy the beautiful nature and the old architecture of the picturesque town!

Top three activities in Fontaineblue:

Visit the chateau!

The chateau was orignially built in the 12th century, and served as a vacation house for royalty!

Go bouldering in the Fontainebleau forest!

The forest is world renowned for it's amazing natural boulders.

Visit a cafe in the lively town center!

Stop for a coffee or some food at one of the many fabulous cafe's in the town, just minutes from the chateau!
